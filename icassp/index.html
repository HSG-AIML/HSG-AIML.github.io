Voise Conversion Demos

Abstract

Zero-shot Voice Conversion (VC) that aims to convert unseen speakers is an attractive topic due to its usefulness in real use-case scenarios. Recent work made progress with disentanglement methods that separate utterance content and speaker characteristics. However, although crucial, extracting disentangled prosody characteristics for unseen speakers remains an issue. In this paper, we propose a novel self-supervised approach to effectively learn abut the prosody information. Then, we use the learned prosodic representations to train our VC model for zero-shot conversion. Our evaluation demonstrates that we can efficiently extract disentangled prosody representation. Moreover, we show improved performance compared to the state-of-the-art VC models.
